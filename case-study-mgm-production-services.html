Case Study MGM Production Services

About

MGM Production Services is a team of talented production professionals that helps bring a wide range of events to life in some of the world’s most beautifully functional event spaces. They're a production company that has built rock solid relationships with every department at every MGM Resorts International® destination, the machine behind Vegas’ most renowned and globally revered resorts.

Objective

The client wanted to bring awareness that MGM Resorts isn’t only a place to book your resort stay, but also has a dedicated staff to book the most beautiful events. With an archive of past events and offerings of different type of production services, my client wanted to build a digital presence that featured what they had to offer while incorporating their branding.

How the Goal was Achieved

  • - Incorporated their gorgeous photography from past events as main header images throughout all the pages and as smaller thumbnail images that linked to other pages.
  • - Incorporated their green brand color and brand font
  • - Vegas is extremely active at night and the dark color scheme is used to subtly symbolize this
  • - Featured dedicated image galleries and video galleries to really show the team’s talent in producing top notch events.
