Patience is the Key

Anything worth building, making, creating, or doing takes time. In this world of instant gratification it can be hard. If you buy something online you can get it at your doorsteps the next day. If you post a picture on a social media site you can see the instant likes. In fact you don't even need to wait anymore to get a taxi. Just call a Uber, and you're set. The way that technology has evolved makes it more and more tempting to be impatient; however, don't be fooled. Patience is the key. If you want that meaningful relationship, it's patience. If you want to be healthy and fit, it's patience. If you want to be incredible at a certain skill, it's patience. Patience is more important than ever in this instant gratification society we live in today.
