Never Give Up

Do you ever get frustrated that you can't do something the way you want to? Maybe it's a new hobby you started. Maybe it's a skill you have to learn for your career. Whatever it may be, is it something you've gotten frustrated about?

From my experience I can assure you that you will achieve it. Just because you may be horrible at what you want to achieve now does not mean you will be horrible in three, twelve, twenty-four months. What I've learned is that there is no such thing as overnight success. Don't expect to be great at something you recently tried.

I think when you try something new and want to be good at something, it is a lot like starting a new relationship or exercise. With both of these, you need to work at them consistently in order to see results. You don't start going to the gym and think you're going to be incredibly fit the next day, do you? You don't start a start dating someone and the next day plan a wedding, do you? Doing anything takes time. I hate to say that to those who are impatient, but it's true. The good news is that what you want to achieve will happen. You will get there. You just have to have patience. If it's something that you really want, never give up.
