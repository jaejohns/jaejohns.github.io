Don't let failure go to your heart

I don’t think the idea of failure and what it actually means gets talked about enough. Traditionally there is a huge negative connotation to failure, which makes people want to avoid failing like it is the plague. If we can change the perception of failure from something bad to something essential, I believe we can be bolder in our decisions which in turn will lead to more fulfilling outcomes.

Will Smith states, “Don’t let failure go to your heart”. Failure is a necessary component of success. Success cannot exist without failure. You cannot achieve a meaningful success without going through failure, so please don’t let yourself be crippled by failure. It is failure that illuminates the path to success.
